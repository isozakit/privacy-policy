Privacy Policy for PulMo

Information Collection

We do not directly collect any personal information from users of our app. When you download and use our app, we do not require you to provide any information about yourself or your device.

However, our app uses third-party services, which may collect data to provide better services.

Third-Party Services

Advertisements and Feature Access

Some features in our app can be unlocked by watching advertisements. These advertisements are provided through third-party services, such as AdMob by Google.

When you watch an ad, the ad provider may collect anonymous data, such as your device information and advertising ID, to deliver targeted advertisements. This data collection is subject to the ad provider's privacy policy. For more information, please review AdMob’s privacy policy:

  • AdMob Privacy Policy

Watching ads to unlock features is entirely optional, and users can choose not to watch ads without affecting the core functionality of the app.

Compliance with COPPA

Our app complies with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us so we can promptly delete it.
